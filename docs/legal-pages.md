**Effective Date: January 1, 2026**  
**Nexo Research | Zagreb, Croatia, EU | OIB: 00000000000**

#### 1. Definition of Research Use Only (RUO) Status

All products sold by Nexo Research are designated as **Research Use Only (RUO)** materials. This designation means:

- Products are intended solely for laboratory research, in vitro experimentation, and analytical chemistry purposes
- Products have NOT been evaluated, tested, or approved by the European Medicines Agency (EMA), the Croatian Agency for Medicinal Products and Medical Devices (HALMED), the U.S. Food and Drug Administration (FDA), or any other regulatory authority for human or veterinary use
- Products are NOT intended for use in diagnostic procedures, clinical trials, therapeutic applications, or any application involving human subjects or animals

#### 2. Eligible Purchasers

Products may only be purchased by:

- Individuals aged 18 years or older
- Qualified research scientists, laboratory professionals, and researchers acting within the scope of their professional duties
- Academic institutions, research organizations, pharmaceutical companies, and registered laboratories
- Any individual or entity conducting lawful laboratory research in compliance with applicable local, national, and EU regulations

Nexo Research reserves the right to request proof of research affiliation or institutional membership and to refuse any order at its sole discretion.

#### 3. Prohibited Uses

The following uses are **strictly prohibited**:

- Human consumption of any kind, including oral, injectable, transdermal, intranasal, or any other route of administration
- Veterinary use or administration to any animal
- Use as food additives, dietary supplements, or cosmetic ingredients
- Diagnostic or prognostic applications
- Therapeutic, prophylactic, or palliative applications
- Any use not consistent with legitimate laboratory or research purposes

#### 4. Documentation Included with Every Order

Nexo Research provides the following documentation with every order:

- **Certificate of Analysis (COA)**: Third-party or in-house HPLC analysis confirming purity ≥99%
- **Safety Data Sheet (SDS)**: Comprehensive handling, storage, and emergency response information
- **Batch Number**: Unique identifier for full traceability
- **Storage Instructions**: Temperature, light, and handling requirements
- **Expiration Date**: Shelf life under specified storage conditions

#### 5. Purchaser Obligations

By completing a purchase, the purchaser agrees to:

1. Store products under conditions specified in the SDS and storage instructions
2. Handle products in accordance with appropriate laboratory safety protocols (PPE, ventilation, etc.)
3. Not resell products for human consumption, veterinary, cosmetic, or therapeutic purposes
4. Comply with all applicable local, national, and EU laws and regulations regarding research chemicals
5. Maintain appropriate records of use consistent with good laboratory practice
6. Not transfer or provide products to third parties who have not agreed to these terms

#### 6. Right to Refuse Orders

Nexo Research reserves the right to refuse, cancel, or refund any order at its sole discretion, including but not limited to orders where:

- The purchaser has not confirmed research-only intent
- There is reasonable suspicion of non-research use
- The shipping destination presents regulatory concerns
- Payment cannot be verified

#### 7. Legal Framework

This policy is governed by the laws of the Republic of Croatia and applicable European Union regulations, including but not limited to Regulation (EC) No 1272/2008 (CLP Regulation) and relevant REACH provisions.
